Outline

The Salesforce Integration for Recotap connects your Salesforce org to the Recotap platform to power your account-based strategy. The Salesforce integration is bi-directional and allows you to:

  • Import contacts, leads, and accounts from Salesforce easily into Recotap to run advertising campaigns.
  • Push Recotap-generated engagement data from Recotap to Salesforce, allowing your sales team to track how different accounts engage with the advertising campaigns and which accounts show intent.

Please note that you can only connect one Salesforce account per Team in Recotap.

In this article:

  • How To Connect Your Salesforce Account With Recotap?
  • Understanding The Salesforce Integration Screen In Recotap

How To Connect Your Salesforce Account With Recotap?

Step 1: Click “Settings” from the left menu of your Recotap dashboard. Select “Integrations” from the drop-down menu that appears.

Step 2: Click “Install” below Salesforce.

Step 3: Click “Connect.” This will take you to the Salesforce login page.

Step 4: Enter your Salesforce Username and Password and select “Log In.”

Note: You may be asked to enter a verification code after entering your Salesforce Username and Password to complete the integration process.

Now the integration is completed. You will be taken to the Recotap Integration dashboard, where you can see Salesforce under the “Installed” section. You can click on the edit icon (pencil icon) to configure your Salesforce integration.

Understanding The Salesforce Integration Screen In Recotap

1. Settings

The Settings screen helps you configure all the rules required for the bi-directional data exchange between Recotap and Salesforce for the following entities:

  1. People
  1. Leads
  1. Accounts

You can choose to import one or all of the entities from Salesforce to Recotap. If you import People and Leads, then Accounts will automatically get imported. But if you just import Accounts, People and Leads will not get imported.

A) How To Import Contacts From Salesforce To Recotap?

You can import your Salesforce Contacts into Recotap by adding a rule.

Step 1: Select “Salesforce contact list” in the ADD RULE box. You can select the contact list(s) from the drop-down menu which you want to import into Recotap.

Step 2: Select a destination in Recotap from “People Only,” “New Segment,” or “Existing Segment” and click “Save.”

  • Choose People Only when you wish to import contact list(s) generically into Recotap.
  • Choose “New Segment” if you want to import your contacts into a new People Based Static or Audience Segment. If you select “New Segment” as the destination, you will have to create a new People Based Static segment. Click “Name your segment” to create a new segment. Add the name of the segment you wish to create and click “Save.” A new segment will be created, and your contact list(s) will be imported into the segment you just created.

  • Choose “Existing Segment” if you already have an existing People-Based Static Audience Segment created in Recotap and wish to import contacts in any of them. If you select “Existing Segment” as the destination, you will have to select an existing People Based Static segment to import your contact list(s). Click “Select Field” and choose the segment from the drop-down menu you want to import the contact list(s). Once done, click “Save.”

Note: If you cannot see the Salesforce contact list(s), it might be because you don’t have permission to view the list. In that case, you may ask your Salesforce administrator for the required permissions.

B) Actions That Can Be Taken On A Rule

Hover over the three-dot menu beside a Source or Destination in a Rule. You will get two options:

  • Edit: If you want to edit a rule, click Edit. Now you can either add a new contact list(s) or delete an existing contact list(s). After making the changes, click “Update.”

  • De-Activate: If you want to delete a rule, click De-Activate, and the rule will disappear from your Recotap dashboard.

Note: Changes made to the People list in Recotap will not be re-written back into the Salesforce account.

C) How To Export People Insights From Recotap Into Salesforce?

Recotap lets you export the Engagement Score of your contacts along with the Last Engagement Date to Salesforce.

The setting for exporting the Engagement Score and Last Engagement Date of your contacts to Salesforce is enabled by default.

Select “Disabled” from the top right corner if you wish to disable this setting. The Export People Insights section will become inactive.

Note: The Engagement Score is updated every 24 hours in your Salesforce account.

D) How To Import Leads From Salesforce To Recotap?

Step 2: Select a destination in Recotap from “People Only,” “New Segment,” or “Existing Segment” and click “Save.”

  • Choose People Only when you wish to import lead list(s) generically into Recotap.
  • Choose “New Segment” if you want to import your leads into a new People Based Static or Audience Segment. If you select “New Segment” as the destination, you will have to create a new segment. Click “Name your segment” to create a new segment. Add the name of the segment you wish to create and click “Save.” A new segment will be created, and your lead list(s) will be imported into the segment you just created.

  • Choose “Existing Segment” if you already have an existing People-Based Static or Audience Segment created in Recotap and wish to import leads in any of them. If you select “Existing Segment” as the destination, you will have to select an existing segment to import your lead list(s). Click “Select Field” and choose the segment from the drop-down menu to which you want to import the lead list(s). Click “Save.”

Note: If you cannot see the Salesforce lead list, it might be because you don’t have permission to view the list. In that case, you may ask your Salesforce administrator for the required permissions.

2. Field Mapping

Field mapping lets you configure which fields in Salesforce map to which field in Recotap. Only the fields you select to map will be populated from your Salesforce account to Recotap.

Select “Field Mapping” beside the “Settings” option from the top menu. Now you will be taken to the Field Mapping screen in Recotap.

A) How To Map A Field?

Step 1: Click on the Select Field drop-down. Select the appropriate field you wish to map from the drop-down menu.

Step 2: Select “Update.”

Note:

  • The Email field in People Sync and Lead Sync and Domain field in Account Sync are mapped by default.
  • If the Email of a particular People or Lead is not filled in your Salesforce account, then that People or Lead is not imported in Recotap.
  • Similarly, if the Website of a particular Account is not filled in your Salesforce account, then that Account is not imported in Recotap.
