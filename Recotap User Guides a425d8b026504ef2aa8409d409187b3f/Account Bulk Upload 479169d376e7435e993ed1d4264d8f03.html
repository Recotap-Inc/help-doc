Target Accounts

The target accounts are the accounts your company is targeting in your ABM program. The target accounts are the best fit accounts that you want to turn into customers.

  • Login to your Recotap account, in the Client panel, navigate to Identify in the navigation bar.
  • Click on Target Accounts in the sidebar menu.

A list of pre-uploaded target accounts appears along with some important details like the industry to which they belong, other insights like the total interactions had, the engagement score, and the date it was last updated.

  • To filter a target account from the list, enter the name of the target account in the search bar placed at the top-left corner of the table.
  • To add a new account as a target account, click on the Add Account button.

Add New Accounts(in bulk)

Add Account facilitates you to import new accounts as target accounts for your company. To make the process convenient and time-saving, the accounts can be added in bulk and need not be imported one at a time.

  • Click on the Add Account button.
  • Select Bulk Upload in the drop-down menu. This will enable you to manually upload the selected target accounts in bulk.
  • To proceed further, download the CSV file template by clicking on the Download template link.
  • Open the downloaded file and fill in the required fields like Name of the Account, Domain name, Industry to which it belongs, Annual Revenue, Employee Size, and Location and save it as a CSV file.
  • Now to upload the CSV file, trackback to your Recotap account, and click on the Select CSV file option.
  • Select the CSV file to be uploaded and click on Submit.

After completing the above instructions, you will be able to upload accounts in bulk.
