Google Conversion Tracking Pixel setup

1) Go to the company's Google Ads account & click on Tools & Settings. Click on Conversions under Measurement tab.

2) Click on the "+" button when you are in this page.

3) Click on the "Website" option.

4) Select the category as Page View. Enter rest of the fields as per your choice & plan. Then click on Create & Continue.

5) Select Install the tag yourself option. Choose the middle option in terms of Google site tag i.e. the google tag is already installed on all pages.

6) Copy the Event snippet/pixel & send it to us for the next steps for conversion tracking in Recotap.
