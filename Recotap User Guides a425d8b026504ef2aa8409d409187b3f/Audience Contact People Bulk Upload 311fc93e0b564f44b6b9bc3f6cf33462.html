Target Audience

The target audience is formed by the set of contacts that belong to the target accounts that your company is targeting in your ABM program. The contacts are the subsets of these accounts and they are directly associated with these accounts. They are also the key decision-makers in each buying group within your target accounts.

  • Login to your Recotap account, in the Client panel, navigate to Identify in the navigation bar.
  • Click on Target Accounts in the sidebar menu.

A list of pre-uploaded target audience appears along with some important details like the account to which they belong, their title, and insights like the total interactions and, the engagement score.

  • To filter a target audience from the list, enter the name of the contact in the search bar placed at the top-left corner of the table.
  • To add a new audience as a target audience, click on the Add Audience button.

Import New Audience(in bulk)

Add Audience facilitates you to import a new audience as the target audience for your company. To make the process convenient and time-saving, the audience can be added in bulk and need not be imported one at a time.

  • Click on the Add Audience button.
  • Select Bulk Upload in the drop-down menu. This will enable you to manually upload the selected target audience in bulk.
  • To proceed further, download the CSV file template by clicking on the Download template link.
  • Open the downloaded file and fill in the required fields like Contact Name, their Company name, their title, email address, Contact number, domain name, LinkedIn url, country to which they belong, and save it as a CSV file.
  • Now to upload the CSV file, trackback to your Recotap account, and click on the Select CSV file option.
  • Select the CSV file to be uploaded and click on Submit.

After completing the above instructions, you will be able to upload the audience in bulk.
