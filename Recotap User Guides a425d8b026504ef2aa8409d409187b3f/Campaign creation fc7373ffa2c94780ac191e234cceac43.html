Campaign creation

Ad campaigns are a set of advertisements that focus on a single message, which are intended to meet a specific business goal as well as lead to conversions.

A creative is the ad served to users on a webpage, app, or other digital environments. Creatives can be images, videos, audio, and other formats that get delivered to users.

To initiate the process of adding Campaigns, follow the steps given below:

  • Login to your Recotap account, in the Client panel, navigate to Target in the navigation bar.
  • Click on Advertising in the sidebar menu.

The overview screen provides a summary of the various campaigns that are running under your name. It provides an ample amount of information about your campaigns like the number of campaigns, the number of accounts it is targeting, the number of impressions recorded, the number of clicks, visits, conversions, etc.

The campaigns screen provides the summary of all the campaigns in a table. It also provides information like media cost, impressions, average CPM, the number of clicks, etc about the various campaigns that are running for your company.

  • Click on Campaigns.

The campaign screen appears with a table showing all the campaigns along with their type, status, the number of creatives present in the campaign, the media cost incurred, and other important metrics useful to understand and measure your marketing efforts.

Add a New Campaign

Now to add a new campaign click on the Add Campaign button on the top-right corner of the screen.

The subsequent screen will display the various steps involved in adding a full-fledged campaign for your company.

  1. Campaign Options: The first step is selecting the campaign options. This screen provides a summary of the new campaign.

It starts with the name of the New Campaign.

  • Add the name of the campaign.

The start date is the date from which the campaign goes live and the end date marks the end of that particular campaign.

  • Select the start and the end date by clicking on the calendar icon and selecting the desired dates.
  • If the end date is not confirmed or you wish to run the campaign for an infinite time, click on the Run Indefinitely option, just below the end date.

The timezone option provides you with different time zones followed across the globe. You can select the timezone of your target audience.

  • Click on the downward-facing arrow. A dropdown menu opens with the various time zones. Select the time zone of the area you wish to target with this new campaign.

Next is the Campaign Type. The creatives to be uploaded as a part of the campaign could be of various types- display, video, native, pop, or push.

  • Select the campaign type from the dropdown menu according to the format and type of creatives to be uploaded for the new campaign.

You can also decide the daily budget.

To save the details and proceed further, click on the Save and Continue button situated at the bottom-right corner of the screen.

2. Audience & Target: The following step is to select the target audience for your campaign. Your target audience refers to the specific group of consumers most likely to want your product or service, and therefore, the group of people who should see your ad campaigns.

The first step is audience segmentation. Audience segmentation is a marketing strategy based on identifying subgroups within the target audience in order to deliver more tailored messaging for stronger connections.

  • To select the audience segment, click on Select Audience. A dropdown menu appears. Select the audience based on your campaign

The second step is to choose the various criteria for targeting and segmenting the selected audience.

There exist three radio buttons:

All: If you wish to select all the options that exist under that particular criteria, then select all.

White list: If you wish to select the suitable options from the complete list of options and make them as your target audience, then select white list.

After you choose the option of white list, a dropdown menu appears. Browse or type the name of the option you wish to select for that particular criteria.

Black list: If you wish to discard or blacklist any options from the list of options that exist under particular criteria, then select black list.

After you choose the option of black list, a dropdown menu appears. Browse or type the name of the options you wish to blacklist for that particular criteria.
