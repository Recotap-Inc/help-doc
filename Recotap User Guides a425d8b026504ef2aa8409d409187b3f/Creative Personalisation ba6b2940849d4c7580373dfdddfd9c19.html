Creative Personalisation

Recotap can help scale your ad personalization efforts by minimizing the manual effort involved in generating your ad copies.

You will be able to generate hundreds of creative variations automatically. Here are the steps to achieve it.

Step 1: Prepare the base creatives

The base creatives are nothing but the creative templates that you would use to imprint your ad copies.

Here are some sample base creatives in different dimensions.

Step 2: Imprint your copy/message to the template

Recotap will use the sample creative to determine the position and style of the texts while generating the creative variations.

Please note Recotap requires a sample ad for all the dimensions for the automatic creative generation process to work.

For example, if you want to personalize ads for fifty accounts across five different ad dimensions, you will have to supply five sample creatives for one account. And Recotap will generate variations for the rest of forty-nine accounts (245 variations).

Step 3: Share the personalized message

In the final step, you will need to supply us with ad copies for all the accounts you require personalized creatives in the below format.
